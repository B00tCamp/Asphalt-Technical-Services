What We Do

Asphalt Technical Services (Pty) Ltd is a SANAS Accredited Civil Engineering Materials Testing Laboratory (Accreditation no. T0876) and highly committed to do all testing activities according to the general requirements of ISO/IEC 17025:2017 International Standard

• The Laboratory operates completely independent from the Group of Companies and seeks to do business with external / commercial customers
• The testing services provided by the Laboratory are diverse and covers the following testing sections:

  • a) Aggregate Testing
  • b) Asphalt Testing
  • c) Bitumen Testing
  • d) Soils & Gravel Testing
  • e) On-site Testing & Sampling
  • f) Specialised Research Testing
